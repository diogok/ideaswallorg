Welcome to The IdeasWall.org, a place where you can deposit your ideas, todo lists, projects and compromisses and access it wherever you are. It's your on-the-go list organizer, ideas management, so you don't have to miss any thought because you do not have where to place it.

Using latest technologies, its a service web accessible, it offer diferents interfaces, proper to any use, and rapid grow in functions.

Now available we have a Web Manager, for a rich experience wherever you are, there is also a rich desktop interface powered by JavaFx, lastest improvement in Rich interfaces, that allow flexible control over your lists. At last, but not least, there is a Mobile Application, to use on your mobile wherever you are, offline or online.

News and support forum can will found at project kenai page.
